I built MarketLens IQ, a browser-based dashboard studio that turns Excel, IMS, and IQVIA-style datasets into professional BI dashboards.

What it does:

- Ingests Excel/CSV files, including large IMS/IQVIA-style workbooks
- Detects pharma market fields like brand, pack, manufacturer, group, therapy, and chronic/acute
- Generates executive, marketing, pre-sales/BD, finance, sales, and custom dashboard views
- Helps teams identify market leaders, whitespace, budget allocation focus, sales gaps, and brand opportunities
- Exports dashboards as PPT, PDF, CSV, HTML, PNG, and BI model JSON

The goal was to make Excel-to-boardroom reporting fast, local-first, and useful for real corporate workflows.
